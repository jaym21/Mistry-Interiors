About Us

MISTRY INTERIORS is a trusted name in the interior design and construction industry since 1975, specializing in office and home interiors.

From concept to completion, we provide a full-spectrum service for commercial, residential, and industrial spaces. We excel in transforming environments into functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces that reflect the unique vision of our clients.
